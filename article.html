DAIKOKU Parking Area

When it comes to Japanese car-parking culture, we have to mention the "Parking Area" (PA) on the motorway, which as the name suggests is a service area for road users to take a break, and among the many PAs, the most famous one is "Daikoku PA".

As the country with the richest car culture, there are not only JDMs in Big Black PA, but also BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi from Germany, Jaguar, Aston Martin from Britain and even Dodge Challenger and Charger, the representative of American hardcore, basically, no matter what you want to get, or rare enough that you don't think you'll ever have the chance to see them, you can see them all in DAIKOKU PA.

The biggest feature of DAIKOKU PA is that it is an underground car gathering event with no fixed time, you can come whenever you want! The various modification styles that can be seen here, you can also feel on the spot that the owners are studying each other's cars from an appreciative point of view, not because of different ideas on modification and not to each other, instead of enthusiastic exchange of ideas, in the venue will not be heard all the time unreasonable sound of pulling and turning, respect for each other is a common tacit understanding in the DAIKOKU PA.
